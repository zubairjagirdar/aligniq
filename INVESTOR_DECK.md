## Pitch Day Reminders

### What Makes Investors Say "Yes"
1. **Market**: Is it big enough? ($8B ✅)
2. **Team**: Can they execute? (ex-Google, ex-Linear ✅)
3. **Traction**: Do customers want it? (pilots + early adoption ✅)
4. **Timing**: Is now the right time? (AI era ✅)
5. **Capital Efficiency**: Can they grow without huge burn? (bootstrapped to MVP ✅)

### What Kills Deals
1. ❌ Founder doesn't believe in their own mission
2. ❌ Large TAM but tiny SOM (we have $2B SOM in Year 1)
3. ❌ No product (we have production-grade MVP)
4. ❌ Huge burn with no clear path to revenue
5. ❌ Team is misaligned or missing skills

### Likely Questions
- "What happens if Workday copies you?"
  → We'll be acquired for $1B+ before they ship
- "Why not start with enterprises?"
  → Sales cycle too long; we're validating with startups first
- "How will you stay independent?"
  → We won't need to. We'll be too valuable to sell.
